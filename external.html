Current Projects

External Affairs has been working on hosting a Speaker Series, as well as on a variety of smaller projects. These include a Mental Health Awareness campaign, a partnership with the Riverside Conservatory, a CPR Training Course for students, as well as the Civic Engagement and Financial Literacy Courses.

Goals

The External Affairs Department is pursuing a partnership with the 9/11 Memorial, organizing a gender pronoun sticker event during Ally week, and setting up a fundraiser to purchase more instruments for the music department.
