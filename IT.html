Description

The IT department is responsible for all aspects of the Student Union's online operations. We work on projects like Stuyactivities and the stuysu.org site and do our best to serve the needs of the student body.

Current Projects

We have been updating and improving StuyActivities by adding new features and handling user requests regarding IT issues and clubs and pubs.

We are also in the process of renovating the stuysu.org website to create an improved user experience and better support and inform the student body.
