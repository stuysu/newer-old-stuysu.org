Description

The SU Graphic Design provides visual communication and establishes a clear representation of the Student Union. The Department is responsible for working with other departments to spread awareness of events and of important information through designing signes, logos, posters, apparel, web pages, the monthly newsletter, and more.

Current Projects

The Department is currently developping the visual images for BOOgrams and Stuyloween, and is creating guide maps for Parent-Teacher Conferences.
