The Communications Department is responsible for maintaining communication and transparency between the Student Union and the student body. The Department aims to promote complete awareness and accessibility of SU developments to all Stuyvesant students throughout the school year. From monthly newsletters and update videos to constant activity on various social media platforms, the Communications Department ensures that students are being informed of what is going on in both the SU and the school as a whole at all times.

Current Projects

Currently, the Communications Department is working at drafting the monthly SU Newsletters and staying active on social media. The Department has also made tutorial videos on how to use StuyActivities and Talos.

Goals

The Communications Department is looking forward to further bridging the gap between students and the school through clear and relevant communication.
